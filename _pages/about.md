Hello! This is Liqiao Xia. I am currently a Research Assistant Professor in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, HKSAR. Before this, I was a Visiting Research Fellow at the Vienna University of Technology, Austria (OeAD project), and the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, Germany (Helmholtz-HIDA project), and a Postdoctoral Scholar in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Case Western Reserve University, USA. I received his Ph.D. in Industrial and Systems Engineering from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, HKSAR, in 2024 and was a Visiting Graduate Student at the Institute for Manufacturing, University of Cambridge, UK. My research interests include human-robot collaboration, smart manufacturing systems, prognostics and health management, etc.
